Halloween

Michael Myers has yet to die. After around 7+ movies no one has been able to get rid of Michael. As a fan of all the Halloween movies this is starting to get a bit repetitive. If anything I'm team Michael Myers now.

Synopsis

Halloween 1978

On a cold Halloween night in 1963, six year old Michael Myers brutally murdered his 17-year-old sister, Judith. He was sentenced and locked away for 15 years.

But on October 30, 1978, while being transferred for a court date, a 21-year-old Michael Myers steals a car and escapes Smith's Grove. He returns to his quiet hometown of Haddonfield, Illinois, where he looks for his next victims.

Halloween 2018

It's been 40 years since Laurie Strode survived a vicious attack from crazed killer Michael Myers on Halloween night. Locked up in an institution, Myers manages to escape when his bus transfer goes horribly wrong.

Laurie now faces a terrifying showdown when the masked madman returns to Haddonfield, Ill. -- but this time, she's ready for him.

[Halloween Kills]

The nightmare isn't over as unstoppable killer Michael Myers escapes from Laurie Strode's trap to continue his ritual bloodbath. Injured and taken to the hospital, Laurie fights through the pain as she inspires residents of Haddonfield, Ill., to rise up against Myers.

Taking matters into their own hands, the Strode women and other survivors form a vigilante mob to hunt down Michael and end his reign of terror once and for all.

Reviews

After 12 long movies not inlcuding the upcoming one, Michael Myers keeps terrorizing Haddonfield. You'd really think that in this new movie he would finally die and we could all live happily ever after. Of course not, why would they kill off our beloved Michael when he still has more victims lifes too take. Halloween Kills was a great movie but when will we finally kill Michael. This movie was more like a filler really, there was nothing new or exciting about him. No new information was learned besides knowing that Michael looks at himself from the window of his bedroom home for some soul searching. That was basically it, like all other movies people try and kill him and fail miserably. If you do enjoy the movies like I do, I still do recommend you to go watch it. Solid 8/10 in my books.
